How It Works

Getting support shouldn't be complicated. We made it simple, safe, and respectful.

1

Create Your Profile

Tell us what kind of support you're looking for. Your privacy is protected and you can stay anonymous until you're ready.

2

Browse Matches

See peer supporters who match your preferences like language, topics, and communication style. Swipe to connect.

3

Start Chatting

Once matched, chat safely with built-in prompts and boundaries. You're in control of the conversation.

4

Feel Heard

Quick check-ins help us improve your experience. Need more support? We'll guide you to resources.
